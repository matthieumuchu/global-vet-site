About Us

Global Vet (U) Ltd was incorporated on the 14th July 2005 and thereafter, registered and licensed by the National Drug Authority to carry out business of importation and distribution of Veterinary drugs, feed supplements, animal vaccines and equipment.

Vision:

To be a leading provider of animal health solutions and related services in the region

Mission:

To provide quality but affordable animal heath solutions and related services to our customers

Location:

Our company is situated at plot 14/18 Nakivubo Place, Venus Plaza building in the capital city, Kampala but also has a branch in the western region of the country in Mbarara Town, on Plot 90, High Street.

We operate through a wide network of distributors in all towns and business centres of Uganda.

Our market extends to neighbouring countries such as Rwanda, Burundi, South Sudan, Northern Tanzania and Eastern Democratic Republic of Congo.

Over the years we have acquired a wide range of products from major manufactures which has given us competitive advantage in the distribution of animal care products A team of well qualified and motivated staff is always available to offer professional services to our customers.

Business partnership:

The Company procures its supplies from a number of foreign partners with whom we enjoy exclusive representation in Uganda. These include:

  • DYNTEC spol s.r.o Czech Republic
